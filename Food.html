    • Famous Egyptian Dishes

    Koshari

    Koshari is a popular Egyptian street food made from a mixture of lentils, rice, pasta, chickpeas, and fried onions, all topped with a spicy tomato sauce and garlic vinegar. It's a delicious and hearty dish enjoyed by many.

    Ful Medames

    Ful Medames is a traditional Egyptian dish of cooked fava beans seasoned with cumin, olive oil, and often topped with chopped parsley, garlic, onion, lemon juice, and chili pepper. It is typically served with bread and is a staple breakfast in Egypt.

    Molokhia

    Molokhia is a traditional Egyptian soup made from finely chopped jute leaves, often flavored with garlic and coriander. It is usually served with rice or bread and accompanied by meat, such as chicken or rabbit.

    Mahshi

    Mahshi is a popular dish in Egypt consisting of vegetables like zucchini, eggplant, or bell peppers, stuffed with a mixture of rice, herbs, and sometimes minced meat. It is a flavorful and satisfying dish often served during special occasions.

    Baklava

    Baklava is a sweet pastry made of layers of filo dough filled with chopped nuts and sweetened with syrup or honey. While it is enjoyed in many Middle Eastern countries, it is a popular dessert in Egypt as well.
